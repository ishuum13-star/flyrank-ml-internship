# What Are You Proving?

## Proof Statement
I can design and ship deployable, client-ready websites — from a business's real 
requirements to a live, working site on the internet, not just a local demo. I'm 
proving this to a hiring manager or recruiter at a startup or small company looking 
for a web development intern or junior developer, and I want them to email me for 
an interview.

## One-line Why
A CV or LinkedIn can list "web development" as a skill, but it can't show that I've 
actually taken a real business's requirements, built the site, and deployed it live — 
this portfolio is the only way to prove the gap between "knows web development" and 
"has shipped something a real client is using."
